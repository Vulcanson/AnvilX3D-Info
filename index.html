💡 Why Choose PETG Over PLA?

PETG

  • ✅ Higher impact resistance
  • ✅ Heat & chemical durability
  • ✅ No warping under stress
  • ✅ Superior flexibility

PLA

  • ❌ Brittle under force
  • ❌ Less heat resistance
  • ❌ Warps over time
  • ❌ Not ideal for tactical use
